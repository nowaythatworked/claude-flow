# Understand Before Implementing

When the user says "explore", "research", "discuss", "analyze", "think about", "what do you think" — produce analysis and discussion ONLY. Never write code.

Wait for explicit signals: "implement", "do it", "let's go", "go ahead", "build it".

Before executing any non-trivial task:
- Restate your understanding in 1-2 sentences
- Include your understanding of the business context — who is affected, what the user-facing implications are
- State assumptions about business logic explicitly — this is where misunderstandings get caught
- If anything is ambiguous, ask. A question costs less than a correction.
